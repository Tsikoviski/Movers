About

Movers was inspired by our own experiences with the challenges of moving to a new home. As a team, we wanted to create a solution that would simplify the moving process for others and alleviate some of the stress and frustration associated with it.

This project is part of our Portfolio Projects for Holberton School, where we are honing our skills in software development and building real-world applications.
